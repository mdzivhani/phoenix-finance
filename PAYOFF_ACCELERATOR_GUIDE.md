# 💰 Mortgage Payoff Accelerator - Complete Guide

## Overview
The **Mortgage Payoff Accelerator** is a powerful financial tool that helps you quickly pay off your mortgage loan while showing exactly how much interest you'll save.

## Features

### 1. **Multiple Payoff Scenarios**
Compare different strategies to see which works best for you:

#### Standard Payment (Baseline)
- No extra payment
- Shows your current payoff timeline
- Displays total interest you'll pay
- Reference point for all other scenarios

#### Accelerated Payment (Extra Month/Year)
- Automatically calculates 1 extra month of payments per year
- Shows months saved
- Displays interest savings
- Easy to implement - just budget an extra payment once yearly

#### Biweekly Payment Plan
- Pay half your monthly payment every 2 weeks (26 payments/year)
- Results in approximately 1 extra full payment per year
- Aligns with some payday schedules
- Shows long-term savings

#### Custom Extra Payment
- Enter any extra amount you can afford (R100, R500, R1000, etc.)
- Instantly see payoff timeline and savings
- Flexible - adjust as your budget changes
- Real-time calculation

#### Target Payoff Date
- Set a goal year to be mortgage-free
- System calculates required extra payment
- Shows exact payoff date
- Helps with long-term planning

### 3. **Interest Savings Calculation**

- See how much you save with each strategy

### 4. **Current Payment Breakdown**

**Key Point**: Early in the loan, most goes to interest. By adding extra payments, you reduce the balance faster, meaning less interest charged monthly.

## Interest Savings Explained

When you add extra payments:
1. **More goes to principal** - Reduces your balance faster
2. **Less balance = Less interest** - Monthly interest calculated on lower balance
3. **Compounding effect** - Savings grow over time
4. **Months saved** - Loan ends sooner, no more interest accrual
